Our Love Story

First Meet

22 Aug 2011

The composition of nature is very obvious. We first met in a place where most of the people would have met their first love, or at least prayed for one. A Shrikrishna Janmashtami evening at our Peedika Bhagavathi temple Karavaloor.

First Date

Dec 2016

Dates are of course for people who go by the books. For us, whenever we meet we'd likely end up mocking each other, laughing at each other or worst case scenario ending up in a fight. Anyway there are very few times we literally set apart with a smile.

Proposal

Dec 2017

It wasn't official till we decided to open up our heart not to ourselves but to our parents. We didn't had a romantic dine out or a long ride and music playing in the background while I stood on my knees and proposed to her. Obviously our chemistry doesn't work that way.

Enagagement

Dec 15 2024

It was the big announcement. Days of anticipation, manifestation, imaginations and struggle. She ended up in the hospital and I ended up paying bills for that after our big day of engagement. Like the always say.. life comes with ups and downs
